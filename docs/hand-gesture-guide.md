## English

### Overview

The Vietnam 3D Map supports hand gesture controls using your webcam. This feature uses MediaPipe Hands to detect and track hand movements in real-time.

### Getting Started

1. Click the **"Enable Camera"** button in the Controls panel
2. Allow browser access to your webcam when prompted
3. Position yourself so your hands are visible to the camera
4. A small video preview will appear showing hand tracking

### Camera Preview Window

The camera preview window is **draggable** - you can move it anywhere on the screen:

- **Drag**: Click and hold the "Camera" title bar, then drag to any position
- **Position Memory**: Your preferred position is saved and restored on next visit
- **Touch Support**: On mobile/tablet, touch and drag the title bar to move

### Single-Hand Gestures

| Gesture | Action | How to Perform |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| **Open Palm** | Rotate map | Extend all 5 fingers, move hand left/right to rotate horizontally, up/down to tilt |
| **Peace Sign** | Toggle sidebar | Extend index and middle fingers (V sign) to show/hide the sidebar panel |
| **Pinch** | Zoom in/out | Touch thumb and index finger together, move them closer for zoom in, apart for zoom out |
| **Pointing** | Fine rotation | Extend only index finger, move to rotate with precision |
| **Fist** | Reset view | Close all fingers into a fist to reset camera to default position |

### Two-Hand Gestures

| Gesture | Action | How to Perform |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| **Two Pointing Fingers** | Pan/Move map | Point with index finger on both hands, move both hands together to pan the map |
| **Two Peace Signs** | Screenshot | Make peace signs (V) with both hands to capture and download a screenshot |
| **Two Open Palms** | Tilt map | Open both palms (3+ fingers each), move hands up/down to tilt the camera angle |
| **Two Pinches** | Two-hand zoom | Pinch with both hands, move hands apart to zoom in, together to zoom out |
| **Two Fists** | Reset view | Make fists with both hands to reset camera |

### Tips for Best Results

- **Lighting**: Ensure good, even lighting on your hands
- **Background**: A plain background helps with detection accuracy
- **Distance**: Keep hands 30-60cm from the camera
- **Movement**: Make smooth, deliberate movements
- **Stability**: Keep hands steady when not intending to control

### Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Hands not detected | Check lighting, move hands closer to camera |
| Jittery movement | Move more slowly, ensure stable hand position |
| Wrong gesture detected | Make gestures more distinct, extend/close fingers fully |
| Camera not working | Check browser permissions, try refreshing the page |
